About the game

This game is a military tower defence game. The aim of the game is to survive 20 waves within the game with a friend or by yourself. There will be rounds of enemies running towards you base and you will have to defend against them by putting turrets down.
After the 20 rounds you can choose to continue the game and play an infinite version of the game!
